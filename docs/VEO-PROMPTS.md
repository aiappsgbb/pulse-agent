# Pulse Agent — Veo 3.1 Prompts

All clips: 8 seconds, 16:9, no audio (muted in edit — we add our own voiceover + music).

## Brand Guidelines (apply to every clip)

- **Person**: 40-year-old man, bald head, dark beard, wearing a dark casual henley or crew neck
- **Setting**: Modern dark glass office at night (V1) transitioning to dawn (V2, V5). Same office throughout.
- **Monitors**: Show varied M365 content — Teams chat lists with unread badges, Outlook inbox, packed calendar grids, code editors, spreadsheets, documents with charts. Not just code.
- **Color palette**: Cyan, green, amber on near-black. No purple, no bright blue, no warm white.
- **Screen aesthetic**: Faint CRT scanlines, subtle phosphor bloom. Monitors are the only light source.
- **Camera**: Slow, deliberate. No handheld shake.

---

## V1 — Information Overload (opening hook)

```
Overhead shot slowly pushing in on a 40-year-old bald man with a dark beard sitting at a curved desk in a dark glass office at night. He is surrounded by five monitors — one shows a Teams chat list with unread badges, another shows an Outlook inbox overflowing with emails, another shows a packed calendar grid, one shows a code editor, and the last shows a document with charts. All screens display content in cyan and white on dark backgrounds, casting cold blue light across his face. Dozens of translucent holographic notification cards in glowing cyan and amber cascade downward through the air like falling leaves, each suggesting a message preview or calendar alert. The man sits motionless, head slightly bowed, overwhelmed. Shallow depth of field, anamorphic lens flares from amber notifications. Faint CRT scanlines on the monitors.
```

## V2 — Dawn / Agent Awakens (transition to product)

```
Same dark glass office. A single ultrawide monitor on a clean dark desk, facing a floor-to-ceiling window. The 40-year-old bald man with a dark beard is seen from behind, silhouetted. Sunrise time-lapse outside — deep blue shifting to golden amber over a city skyline. The monitor shows a minimal terminal dashboard with a few lines of glowing green and cyan text on a black background — a short clean list, a blinking cursor, a status bar at the bottom. Faint CRT scanlines on the screen. Warm sunrise light creeps across the desk from the right while the monitor glows cool cyan-green from the left. Rack focus from skyline to monitor in final 2 seconds. Calm, resolved.
```

## V3 — Data Streams (abstract, spare clip)

```
Dark abstract space with a faint cyan dot-grid background. Thin ribbons of cyan and green light carrying tiny rectangular shapes flow from multiple directions, converging into a central glowing amber hexagonal node that pulses. Data compresses — many small fragments enter, fewer bright green blocks exit. Particle effects have a slightly pixelated retro quality. Subtle anamorphic lens flares. Slow camera dolly from left to right through the scene.
```

## V4 — Single Agent Architecture (how one agent works)

```
Dark abstract space with a faint cyan dot-grid background. Overhead bird's-eye view. A single large glowing amber hexagonal node sits at the center. From the left, multiple thin cyan data streams flow into it — each stream carries tiny rectangular shapes representing different data sources. From the right, clean green structured blocks flow out of the central node in a single organized stream. Around the central node, smaller cyan tool icons orbit slowly like satellites — fourteen small dots arranged in a ring. Four teal horizontal bars sit below the node like a foundation. One agent, many inputs, structured output. Clean, precise, engineered.
```

## V8 — Agent Network (team-scale collaboration)

```
Dark abstract space with a faint cyan dot-grid background. Overhead bird's-eye view. Twenty small glowing cyan hexagon nodes are arranged in a loose organic cluster, each pulsing gently like a heartbeat. Every node represents one person's agent. Amber data packets travel between nodes along thin teal connection lines — some packets go left to right, others diagonal, others across the cluster, creating a living mesh of knowledge exchange. The packets leave faint teal trails that linger and overlap, forming a web pattern. The cluster slowly breathes — nodes drift slightly outward then inward. No central hub, no hierarchy — a peer-to-peer network of agents sharing knowledge autonomously. Clean, precise, alive.
```

## V5 — Closing (bookend to V1)

```
Same dark glass office as V1, but dawn light now floods through the floor-to-ceiling windows. The 40-year-old bald man with a dark beard stands by the window looking out at the golden sunrise over the city, one hand resting on the desk. Behind him, a single monitor shows a clean terminal dashboard — just a few items in green and cyan text, not the overwhelming wall from before. The holographic notification cards are gone — the air is clear. The monitor still glows behind him, cursor still blinking. Warm amber light from the right, cool cyan glow from the left.
```

## V6 — Transition Bumper (reusable, between scenes)

```
Dark screen with faint CRT scanlines on a near-black background. A horizontal beam of cyan light sweeps rapidly across from left to right like a CRT electron gun. It leaves a phosphor green afterglow that fades over 1 second. Then an amber beam sweeps at a different height. Both afterglows dissolve into pixel-like particles against the dark background.
```

## V7 — Multi-Agent Collaboration (the wow moment)

```
A dark glass office viewed from a wide angle. Two desks sit side by side, each with a monitor showing a terminal dashboard in cyan and green text on black backgrounds. A 40-year-old bald man with a dark beard sits at the left desk. The right desk has a different person — a woman — working at her own terminal. Between the two monitors, a glowing amber data packet materializes in mid-air and travels smoothly from the left screen to the right screen, leaving a teal particle trail that fades behind it. As it arrives, the right monitor flashes briefly with new green text appearing. Then a second amber packet travels back from right to left. Both screens update. Two agents, exchanging knowledge, no words spoken. Faint CRT scanlines on both monitors. The room is lit only by the screens.
```
