# Pulse Agent — Veo 3.1 Prompts

All clips: 8 seconds, 16:9. Consistent style: CRT terminal aesthetic, cyan/green/amber on dark backgrounds, faint scanlines, retro gaming vibe.

---

## V1 — Information Overload (opening hook)

```
Overhead shot of a person at a desk in a dark office at night. Three monitors display dense scrolling text in cyan monospace font on black screens. Dozens of translucent holographic notification cards cascade downward through the air above the desk like falling leaves — glowing cyan and amber rectangles with faint scanline textures. The person sits motionless, overwhelmed. Shallow depth of field, anamorphic lens flares from the amber notifications. Only light source is the monitors. Dark, quiet, heavy.
```

## V2 — Dawn / Agent Awakens (transition to product)

```
A single ultrawide monitor on a clean dark desk, facing a floor-to-ceiling window. Sunrise time-lapse outside — deep blue shifting to golden amber. The monitor shows a minimal terminal with a few lines of glowing green text and a blinking cursor on a black background, faint CRT scanlines. Warm sunrise light creeps across the desk from the right while the monitor glows cool cyan-green from the left. Rack focus from skyline to monitor in final 2 seconds. Calm, resolved.
```

## V3 — Data Streams (abstract transition)

```
Dark abstract space. Thin ribbons of cyan and green light carrying tiny rectangular shapes flow from multiple directions, converging into a central glowing amber hexagonal node that pulses. Data compresses — many small fragments enter, fewer bright green blocks exit. Faint dot-grid background in dim teal. Particle effects have a slightly pixelated retro quality. Subtle lens flares. Electronic ambient hum.
```

## V4 — Architecture Map (technical credibility)

```
Overhead bird's-eye shot of a network diagram assembling on a dark surface. Glowing cyan hexagon nodes appear on the left, a large amber circle pulses in the center, green rectangle nodes appear on the right. Thin teal connection lines draw themselves between nodes with tiny particles flowing along them like data packets. Faint graph-paper grid in dim teal on the background. Slow subtle rotation. Clean, precise, engineered.
```

## V5 — Closing (bookend to V1)

```
Same dark office as V1, but dawn light now floods through the windows. The desk has one monitor showing a clean terminal dashboard — just a few items in green and cyan text, not the overwhelming wall of notifications. The notification cards from the opening are gone. A person stands by the window looking out at the sunrise, silhouetted. The monitor still glows behind them — cursor still blinking. Warm amber from the right, cool cyan from the left. Resolution.
```

## V6 — Transition Bumper (reusable, between scenes)

```
Dark screen with faint CRT scanlines. A horizontal beam of cyan light sweeps rapidly across from left to right like a CRT electron gun. It leaves a phosphor green afterglow that fades over 1 second. Then an amber beam sweeps at a different height. Both afterglows dissolve into pixel-like particles. Sharp electronic scan sound. Quick, punchy.
```

---

## Notes

- **Palette**: Cyan, green, amber on near-black. If Veo drifts to other colors, regenerate.
- **All real text** (stats, tagline, lower-thirds) added in Clipchamp post-production. Veo clips just need the *feel* of terminal screens.
- **Most of the video is screen recordings** of the actual TUI. These clips are quick transitions and bookends — not the main content.
- **Generation order**: V6 (bumper) first as a test, then V1+V5 (bookend pair), then V3+V4 (abstract), then V2.
